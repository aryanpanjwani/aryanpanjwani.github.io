Personal

Hey, welcome to my site. If you don't know me, my name is Aryan. Though originally born in Houston, I grew up in Sugar Land, TX.

(Un)fortunately, I have a habit of dabbling in one too many hobbies at a time. Here are a few of my current favorites, followed by an assortment of some pictures.

I started playing basketball when I was pretty young and have been fortunate to be able to continue to do so. I've only mostly played recreationally. It's very calming as once you're on the court, nothing else really comes to mind. Also, it's a pretty social activity. I often find myself talking to my opponents and meeting people I haven't seen in some time.
I took piano lessons when I was very young, but only for a little bit. I remember nearly nothing other than the note names. Recently, I've gotten back into it. I still can't really play anything, so please don't ask lol. It's tough to find time to learn and practice with schoolwork, clubs, etc, but I try to make time here and there because I think it's such an elegant instrument that I want to pick up.
I first played Super Smash Bros on my friend's switch after my AP Gov final my senior year of HS. I never played too much until this past semester. I've found it to be such an enjoyable pastime, since it's a very social game that a lot of people I know tend to play. Although, it can get very competitive at times, I often find myself developing strong connections sitting on a couch alongside a group of friends engaging in friendly banter as we aim to launch each other off of the stage.
I am alright at Chess. After playing it a few times in 4th Grade, I picked it back up recently. Before you ask, my rating is like 900 on a good day. I intentionally have chosen to not go down the rabbit hole and learn openings, tactics, etc. I currently enjoy hopping on Chess.com, getting obliterated, and continuing about my day.
