This course is infamously know throughout UT's McCombs as "Killer K". For the final project in this class, we were tasked with creating a C#-based ASP.NET MVC website purposed for managing student job/internship recruiting with a SQL Server database hosted on Microsoft Azure .

The system we created has three roles: students, recruiters, and CSO staff. Students can apply for positions and attend interviews, recruiters can create jobs and interview students, and CSO staff manage the entire process and serve as super users for the system. This project required >100 total hours of coding, debugging, testing.

I'm grateful for having a persistent and dedicated team through the process, and the experience taught me a lot about myself in terms of grit and resilience. In the end, we managed to win 2nd place out of the 33 competing teams. Check out the site and login as a CSO to get the full experience: Link to Bevo Recruiting
(P.S. We were judged solely on functionality and not aesthetics lol.)
